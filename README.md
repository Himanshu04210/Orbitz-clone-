# Orbitz clone
```It is travelling base website where you can book the hotel, flight, texi and many more factionality are there.```
<p>
    <h3>Description: </h3>
    Travelling has become more convenient with the advent of online booking platforms that allow you to plan your trip from the comfort of your home. There are many websites       that offer travel booking services, but some stand out due to their ability to provide a one-stop-shop for all your travel needs. One such website is a travelling base         website where you can book flights, hotels and taxis as well. This website makes travelling planning a breeze as you can book your entire itinerary in one place. Whether       you are looking for a budget-friendly option or a luxurious stay, you can easily compare prices and select the best option that fits your needs. Additionally, you can also     book taxis to take you to and from the airport or around your destination city. This website takes the hassle out of travel planning, allowing you to focus on enjoying         your trip to the fullest.
    <h3>An individual project completed in 5 working days</h3>
