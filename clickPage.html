About this area

Lucknow

Located in Gomti Nagar neighborhood, Renaissance Lucknow Hotel is connected to a shopping center. Ambedkar Memorial Park and Bara Imambara are notable landmarks, and some of the area's popular attractions include Botanical Gardens and Lucknow Zoo. Military Temple and Dream World are also worth visiting.
